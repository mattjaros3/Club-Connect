Club Archery

Archery welcomes all skill levels. The club serves as an opportunity for students to enjoy their experience of the sport, whether it is to learn for fun or to compete at a national level. Our parent organization is USA Archery, and club equipment can be provided thanks to the USA Archery Collegiate Program Equipment Grant.

Club Football

Pitt Club Football is an NCFA (National Club Football Association) collegiate tackle football team that competes against other collegiate club football teams. Pitt Club Football is a registered student organization and club sport at the University of Pittsburgh open to both the undergraduate and graduate student body..

Computer Science Club

Pitt CSC exists as tool to build a community of students interested in Computer Science, regardless of declared major. It is intended to be a community of members to develop professionally, engage with each other socially, and explore the field of Computer Science intellectually.
